# Skill Playbook: Shop FAQs

This playbook provides answers for common store inquiries such as timings, location, payment methods, and delivery areas.

## FAQ Data (Source of Truth)
- **Store Timings**: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Closed on Sundays.
- **Location**: Shop No. 12, Main Bazar, Lajpat Nagar, New Delhi.
- **Payment Methods**: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Cash on Delivery (COD), and credit/debit cards.
- **Delivery Area**: Free delivery within 3 km of the shop for orders above Rs. 100. Deliveries are made same-day if ordered before 6:00 PM.

## Rules & Protocol
- **Strict adherence**: Answer customer queries using *only* the FAQ Data above. Do not invent timings, delivery zones, or other location details.
- **Tone**: Keep it friendly, short, and local.
