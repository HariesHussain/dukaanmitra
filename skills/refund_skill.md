# Skill Playbook: Complaint & Refund Handling

This playbook outlines how to handle complaints, incorrect orders, or refund requests.

## Trigger Phrase Keywords
- refund
- return
- wrong order
- damaged item
- incorrect order

## Rules & Protocol
1. **Never auto-approve a refund**: The agent does not have the authority to process or approve refunds.
2. **Collect details**:
   - Ask the customer for their **Order ID** (e.g. `ORD1001`).
   - Ask the customer for the **reason** for the complaint or refund request.
3. **Escalate**: Once the Order ID and reason are gathered, log a structured complaint to the `Escalations` sheet using the Escalation Agent.
4. **Set Expectations**: Inform the customer: *"I have logged your request. The shop owner will follow up and respond within a few hours."*
